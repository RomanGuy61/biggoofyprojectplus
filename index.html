— Story —

The world runs on B E A N S.

Not just food — the source of life, energy, and even the laws of physics. Protectors guard them. Scientists study them. Civilizations depend on their balance.

Then Susy stole the Prime Bean. Mountains flicker like broken screens. Forests forget their own shapes. People lose memories mid-sentence.

You and your best friend Grimace, the chaotic purple hero of questionable shape, are the only ones close enough to chase her trail.

But Susy didn't steal it for power. Someone had already tampered with it — Rom, a legendary world-hacker erased from history, leading the corrupted faction known as "the Glitchgang".

"The world deserves the truth."

— Susy's last message
